About Srimini Arts and Cultural Academy

For over a decade, Srimini Arts and Cultural Academy has been a nurturing ground for artistic excellence and cultural enrichment.

With more than 500 students successfully trained and certified, we take pride in being a leading institution in promoting Indian classical arts and fine arts education.

At Srimini, we believe in encouraging young minds to explore their creativity, cultivate discipline, and develop deep-rooted knowledge in traditional and contemporary art forms. Our experienced faculty members are passionate about guiding each student on their unique artistic journey, ensuring a balance of technique, expression, and cultural values.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to advance your skills, Srimini Arts and Cultural Academy is dedicated to empowering students with quality education and an inspiring environment to flourish in their chosen field.

Our Courses

Bharatanatyam

Learn the ancient classical dance form of Tamil Nadu, encompassing intricate footwork, expressive gestures, and spiritual storytelling.

Drawing

Master the fundamentals of drawing, from basic shapes to advanced techniques in perspective and shading.

Veena

Learn to play the divine Saraswati Veena, one of India's most ancient string instruments with a rich musical heritage.

Vocal

Develop your singing voice with classical Carnatic or Hindustani training, or contemporary vocal techniques.

Nattuvangam

Master the art of Nattuvangam - the rhythmic accompaniment and direction in Bharathanatiyam performances.
